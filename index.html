About Me

Professional Writer & Developer

I'm a passionate developer with experience in building web applications and a seasoned writer and researcher with seven years of experience in academic writing. I hold a Communications degree (with a Swahili major) from Moi University, with aspirations to venture into the Medical or Legal field.

As a writing expert, I've created content across diverse fields including Nursing, Sociology, Business, Economics, Law, Government, Biology, History, Philosophy, Psychology, Literature, Religious Studies, Political Science, Music, and English. My portfolio includes research papers, reports, analysis essays, outlines, literature reviews, critical thinking write-ups, and annotated bibliographies.

My years of experience have taught me the importance of thorough research for every writing piece, which boils down to choosing the correct search terms and knowing how to identify credible sources.
